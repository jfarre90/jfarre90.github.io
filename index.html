Personal Summary

I started with Web development as a hobby while I was working as an Automotive Engineer. I loved being able to bring things that were only in my head to life, and make apps that could quickly make a difference to our day to day life.

Since then, I have expanded my knowledge to different coding languages, mobile app development and I am just getting started on machine learning. Therefore, I think it is now the correct moment to start getting my hobby into a profession.

I have the experience of working with an international engineering company for the past 5 years, where I have had to lead teams to achieve the given targets under strict timing. Through that experience, I have learned to work under pressure and to communicate with a wide international team and lead them, as well as prepare quality documentation to cover all possible issues in mass production. Although not directly involved in software/app development, they are one of the most important skills in any business.

I am looking to work with companies that have ambitious projects with a big vision and motivation. If I work for a company, it will be because I also share the vision and ambition, and am willing to put all of my motivation to get that idea into a reality.
